"Hurry Up Tomorrow" Album by The Weeknd


The transitions between songs in this album are seamless. The Weeknd never fails to create masterpieces when it comes to music. Easily one of my top 3 favorite album releases of 2025.

"Coming Home" Album by IC3PEAK


Ethereal is the perfect word to describe this album. A 10/10 album for me and one of the best releases of this year in my opinion.

"Hell Hill" Album by Elita


This album is a whole new experience, I can only describe it as surreal. I'm not sure what Elita's inspiration for this project was, but the sounds and visuals of this entire album remind me of the popular horror franchise Silent Hill. I love this album as it is like no other I've ever heard.
